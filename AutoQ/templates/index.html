AutoQ

Improving reading comprehension through automatic question generation


This site is designed to help improve reading comprehension for English-language learners. Starting with the articles from Wikipedia, we have developed algorithms to produce reading comprehension questions designed to mimic the style of questions seen on the TOEFL exam. Improving reading comprehension stems from practice on new material.
To get started, you can choose a topic, or you can allow us to choose a random topic for you.
